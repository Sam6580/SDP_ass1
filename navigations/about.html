About City General Hospital

Founded to serve the healthcare needs of our community, City General Hospital combines advanced medical practices with a compassionate approach. Our multidisciplinary teams include specialists across medicine, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics, and critical care.

Our Mission

To deliver patient-centered, high-quality healthcare that improves health outcomes and enhances the well-being of the community.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Caring for patients with dignity.
  • Excellence: Maintaining high clinical standards.
  • Collaboration: Working across teams for best outcomes.
  • Safety: Prioritizing patient and staff safety.
