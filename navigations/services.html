Our Services

We provide a wide range of clinical and support services designed to meet the needs of patients across all ages. Below are our core service areas with brief descriptions.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency department with triage, resuscitation, and short-stay observation. Our emergency team handles trauma, acute medical emergencies, and rapid stabilization prior to admission or discharge.

Outpatient Clinics

Specialist clinics by appointment including cardiology, endocrinology, pediatrics, orthopedics, and more. Services include consultations, follow-ups, diagnostics, and outpatient procedures.

Inpatient Care

Short-term and long-term inpatient services with dedicated wards, nursing care, and multidisciplinary care planning for surgeries, medical treatment, and recovery support.

Diagnostics & Imaging

On-site radiology and laboratory services including X-ray, ultrasound, CT, MRI (referral), blood tests, and clinical pathology to support rapid diagnosis.

Pharmacy & Medication Management

Hospital pharmacy dispenses inpatient and outpatient prescriptions, provides medication counselling, and supports pharmacy stock management and expiry tracking.

Laboratory Services

Routine and specialized laboratory testing with timely reporting to clinicians; supports inpatient, outpatient and emergency care.
